My name is Ameen. I’m a huge car enthusiast and eversince I was a little kid I loved cars. Cars have been my life. My passion started off with the love of their looks and how futuristic they were. So over the years, I began watching and learning more and more about them. Till this day I still research about new cars. Unfortunately, I’m still not old enough to own and drive a car but it is my dream to own a super car. I’ve been in one supercar before (Lamborghini Aventador S) and it was the best experience of my life. The person who gave me the ride was an extremely respectful individual and I was truly inspired by his actions. Before the drive, I wasn’t aware of how fun these cars actually were but as soon as I got a ride in one my life changed. Everyday I wake up I’m always thinking about cars.
